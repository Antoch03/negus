Excellent first Ethipian food experience

We came upon this cozy restaurant while searching for vegetarian options and we were certainly not disappointed. The owner was very welcoming and made the meal a fun experience. There are a variety of meat options but our vegetarian choices were fabulous. We left with a new found love of Ethiopian food.

Florence C, Ottawa (Canada)

Plus qu'un d

Plus qu'un diner au restaurant, venir manger au NEGUS c'est s'offrir le temps d'une soirée un aller-retour pour Addis Abeba ! Un véritable voyage pour les papilles, les oreilles, les yeux et... les mains ! J'ai retrouvé l'ambiance de mes repas lors de mon voyage en Ethiopie, l'injera est plus douce au palais que celle que l'on peut manger sur place, mais je ne m'en plaindrais pas... Les garnitures et sauces sont excellentes. Il ne manque plus que le Tej et les azmaris ! L'accueil de Sisay est exceptionnel. Si après ça vous n'avez pas envie d'aller découvrir ce merveilleux pays qu'est l'Ethiopie...

Philipe B - Billiere, France

Small, homely, intimate taste of Ethiopia in Paris

Brought the olds here for their first experience of Ethiopian food. Superb injera to share, Ethiopian beer and a great atmosphere. Not too spicy, though I think we got the beginner's version, and you could probably ask for a bit more chilli if required. We had fresh mango and cinnamon for desert, and traditionally served Ethiopian coffee. The venue is a blink-and-you'll-miss-it adapted living room covered in pictures and artefacts from Ethiopia. Highly recommended.i

Natalie, Townsville (Australia)
